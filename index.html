Privacy Policy for Bids & Bills

Last Updated: September 5, 2025

Your Privacy is Our Priority
Private by Default User-Controlled Backups 100% Yours

Introduction

Bids & Bills by Family Guardian is designed with your privacy as the top priority. All data is stored locally on your device by default. You have full control, including the option to create backups in your own Google Drive account.

Data Collection

We do not collect or share any data with ourselves or third parties.

  • No personal information collected by us
  • No financial data collected by us
  • No usage statistics
  • No analytics or tracking
  • No crash reports
  • No advertising identifiers
  • No device information shared

If you enable Google Drive backups, your app data (for example: bills, receipts, jobs, transactions) is transmitted securely to your own Google Drive account. This transfer is user-initiated and never accessible to us.

Data Storage

How your data is stored:

  • Local storage (default): Bills, transactions, jobs, and events are stored in a local database; settings are stored in your device’s secure storage.
  • Optional cloud backup: You may choose to back up your data to your own Google Drive account. Backups are encrypted in transit and remain private to your Google account.
  • No external servers or developer databases are used.

You have complete control over your data. Uninstalling the app will delete the data stored on your device. You can delete backup files directly from your Google Drive at any time.

Internet & Permissions

The app works fully offline by default.

  • Internet permission is required only if you enable Google Drive backups.
  • No data is sent to our servers or shared with third parties.
  • No online advertisements and no hidden background network activity.
  • All app updates are delivered through the Google Play Store.

Data Security

Your data security measures:

  • Data on your device is protected by Android’s sandbox security.
  • Backups use secure HTTPS transfer to Google Drive.
  • No account system or developer password storage reduces vulnerability.
  • You control your backup files directly in Google Drive.

Your Rights

You have complete control:

  • View and manage all data within the app
  • Edit or delete any information at any time
  • Delete backups from your Google Drive account
  • Delete all on-device data by uninstalling the app

Children’s Privacy

This app is safe for users of all ages:

  • We do not collect data from anyone, including children
  • No social features or communication capabilities
  • No in-app purchases or advertisements

Policy Updates

We may update this privacy policy occasionally:

  • Updates will be included in app releases
  • The “Last Updated” date will change
  • Our core promise remains: we do not collect or share your data

Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy or the app:

Developer: Family Guardian
Email: familyguardian91@gmail.com

  • Please include “Bids & Bills” in the subject line
  • We typically respond within 48–72 hours

For technical issues, please include:

  • Device model and Android version
  • Description of the issue
  • Steps to reproduce (if applicable)
In Summary
We don’t collect, store, share, or sell your data. Everything stays private on your device, with optional user-controlled Google Drive backups. You remain in complete control.
