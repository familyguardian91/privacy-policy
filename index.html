Privacy Policy for Billing Beta Test

Last Updated: January 10, 2025

Your Privacy is Our Priority
100% Local 100% Private 100% Yours

Introduction

Billing Beta Test by Family Guardian is designed with your privacy as the top priority. This app operates entirely on your device with no internet connection required and no data ever leaving your phone.

Data Collection

We DO NOT collect any data. Period.

  • No personal information
  • No financial data
  • No usage statistics
  • No analytics or tracking
  • No crash reports
  • No advertising identifiers
  • No device information

All your data stays on your device and is never transmitted anywhere.

Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device:

  • Bills, transactions, and events are saved in a local database
  • Settings are stored in your device's secure storage
  • No cloud backup or sync features
  • No external servers or databases
  • Data is only accessible by this app on your device

You have complete control over your data. Uninstalling the app will permanently delete all stored information.

Internet & Permissions

This app does NOT require internet permission and will never:

  • Connect to any servers
  • Send or receive network data
  • Download updates without your consent
  • Share information with third parties
  • Display online advertisements

The app works 100% offline. Any future updates will be delivered through the Google Play Store only.

Data Security

Your data security measures:

  • All data is protected by your device's built-in security
  • We recommend using device lock screen protection
  • Data is isolated to this app using Android's sandbox security
  • No data transmission means no interception risk
  • No account system means no password vulnerabilities

Your Rights

You have complete control:

  • View all your data within the app
  • Edit or delete any information at any time
  • Export your data (coming in future updates)
  • Delete all data by uninstalling the app
  • No need to request data deletion - you control it all
  • No need for data portability requests - it's already yours

Children's Privacy

This app is safe for users of all ages:

  • We don't collect data from anyone, including children
  • No online features that could expose children to risks
  • No social features or communication capabilities
  • No in-app purchases or advertisements

Policy Updates

We may update this privacy policy occasionally:

  • Updates will be included in app updates

  • Major changes will be noted in update descriptions
  • Our commitment to local-only data will never change
